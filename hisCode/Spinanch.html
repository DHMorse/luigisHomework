Health Benefits of Spinach

Why eating spinanch is so good for you.

Popeye was certainly justified in his love for spinach. This green, leafy vegetable is rich in certain nutrients that promote healthy vision, like lutein and zeaxanthin; carotenoid pigments that accumulate in the retina of the eye. Lutein and zeaxanthin are capable of absorbing blue light and can prevent a considerable amount of blue light from reaching structures of the eye involved in vision.
